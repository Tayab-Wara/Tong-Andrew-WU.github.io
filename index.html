About Me

Tong Wu (B.Eng'17, PhD'21) is currently a tenure-track Assistant Professor in ECE at the University of Central Florida. My current research interest includes reinforcement learning, graph signal processing and universal graph neural network in the network systems, especially power networks.

Research Interests

My research interests lie in machine learning, signal processing and optimization in network systems, particularly in

  • power networks;

  • wireless communications;

  • brain networks.
