About Me

Tong Wu (B.Eng'17, PhD'21) is currently a Postdoctoral Associate in ECE at Cornell University, based in New York City, located at the Cornell Tech Campus. My current research interest includes reinforcement learning, graph signal processing and graph neural network in the network systems, especially power networks, wireless communications, and brain networks. My current favorite food includes hot pots and dumplings.

Research Interests
